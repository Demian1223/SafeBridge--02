Frequently Asked Questions at SafeBridge

Medication Questions

What should I do if I miss a dose of my medication?

If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember. If it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and resume your usual dosing schedule. Do not double up to make up for a missed dose.

Can I take medications with food or drinks?

Some medications should be taken with food to reduce stomach upset, while others should be taken on an empty stomach for better absorption. Check the label or consult your doctor or pharmacist for specific instructions.

What are the possible side effects of my medication?

Side effects vary depending on the medication. Common side effects include nausea, dizziness, and headaches. For a complete list of side effects, refer to the information leaflet or consult your healthcare provider.

How should I store my medication?

Most medications should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Some may require refrigeration. Always keep medications out of reach of children.

Shopping Questions

How do I place an order online?

To place an order, browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will be prompted to enter your shipping details and payment information to complete the purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. You can also use gift cards or store credits if available.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the carrier’s website.

What is your return policy?

You can return most items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Please refer to our return policy page for more details.

Services Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of services including primary care, specialized treatments, surgical procedures, and wellness programs. Our services are designed to meet the healthcare needs of individuals and families.

Do you offer telehealth services?

Yes, we offer telehealth consultations that allow you to speak with our healthcare providers from the comfort of your home. You can book a telehealth appointment through our website or by calling our office.

How do I know which service is right for me?

Our healthcare professionals can help you determine which services are best suited to your needs. You can schedule a consultation to discuss your symptoms and receive personalized recommendations.

Are your services covered by insurance?

Many of our services are covered by insurance. We recommend checking with your insurance provider or contacting our billing department for specific details regarding coverage.

Shipping and Delivery Questions

How long will my order take to arrive?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping can take 2-3 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping fees and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please check our shipping policy for more details.

What should I do if my order hasn’t arrived?

If your order hasn’t arrived by the estimated delivery date, please check the tracking information provided. If the tracking shows a delay or if the package appears lost, contact our customer service for assistance.

Can I change the delivery address after placing an order?

You can change the delivery address if your order hasn’t been shipped yet. Contact our customer service as soon as possible to make the change.
