Meet Our Dedicated Doctors at SafeBridge

Milan, Italy - SafeBridge Founding Hospital

Dr. Alessandro Rossi

Dr. Alessandro Rossi has served as the Chief Medical Officer at SafeBridge Italy for over a decade. He holds an MD from the University of Milan and a Master’s in Healthcare Management. Dr. Rossi is dedicated to ensuring top medical standards across SafeBridge.

Dr. Francesca Bianchi

Dr. Francesca Bianchi, Medical Director at SafeBridge Italy, has spent 15 years with the institution. She graduated from Sapienza University of Rome and specializes in internal medicine. Dr. Bianchi’s leadership has significantly enhanced the quality of patient care at SafeBridge.

Dr. Marco Verdi

Dr. Marco Verdi, Head of Cardiology at SafeBridge Italy, has worked with SafeBridge for 12 years. He earned his MD from the University of Padua and specializes in cardiology. Dr. Verdi is dedicated to improving heart health and advancing cardiovascular treatments.
